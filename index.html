About Me

For as long as I can remember, I've always had an affinity towards computers. Deviating from my family's medical school aspirations, I followed my own passions and eventually graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Information Technology. During my courses, I became engrossed by programming side of IT as I started to teach myself programming concepts online, and am now enrolled in the Java coding bootcamp at Tech Elevator. After I graduate, I’m looking for a junior developer position that allows me to leverage my analytical and professional experience. I get most excited about working at an innovative organization with a strong emphasis on collaboration and can’t wait to solve problems for a living.
